Wentao Zhang

Research Associate · Nanyang Technological University

Hi! I am Wentao Zhang, currently a Research Associate at Nanyang Technological University, advised by Prof. Bo An. Prior to this, I worked as a Research Engineer at Skywork AI 2050 Research (Nov 2024 – Feb 2026), where I built LLM-powered multi-agent systems. I received my M.S. in Computer Science from Nanjing University and my B.S. from Tianjin University of Finance and Economics.

My research focuses on LLM-powered autonomous agents and Financial AI (AI4Finance) — building hierarchical multi-agent frameworks for complex, long-horizon tasks (web navigation, computer control, data analysis) and applying reinforcement learning and large language models to algorithmic trading and portfolio management.

Keywords: LLM Agents · Multi-Agent Systems · AI4Finance · Reinforcement Learning · Quantitative Trading
